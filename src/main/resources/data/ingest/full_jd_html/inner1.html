Job description

Job Summary

The Software Engineer will design, build and implement software applications and provide support for agency business software platforms. He/she will have an expertise in application integration and web technologies.

Duties & Responsibilities
  • Build, test and implement solutions as needed to solve business problems or meet business needs
  • Perform software testing, quality assurance, performance tuning, usability and automation of business applications
  • Manage relationship with platform vendors
  • Install and support platform hardware and software
  • Manage security administration for within software platforms
  • Create training materials and provide ongoing training to the agency
  • Provide ongoing support and documentation of platforms with technology support staff
  • Other duties as may be determined.

  • Competencies & Skills Needed

    Customer service oriented; detail oriented and organized; thorough knowledge of standard concepts, practices and procedures used in application development and support; workflow and process analysis and design; project management; collaborative; knowledge of various software languages/platforms including but not limited to Java, C#, MVC Framework, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, RESTful APIs, VB script, PHP, MySQL, SQL, MongoDB, Linux CLI, Windows Server

    Education & Experience Needed

    At least 2 years relevant experience as a software engineer, including analyzing, designing and building workflows and processes, programming, security administration, user support and training, problem management and resolution, and installation and support of hardware and software.
